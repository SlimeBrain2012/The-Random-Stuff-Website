How To Get Games On A School Laptop!

How do you get blocked games on your school laptop?
The answer is suprisingly simple, but you need something before you start

  • What you need
  • A school laptop
  • A USB stick
  • A laptop/computer with the game of choice on it

    • Now that you have got everything, lets get started!

    1) Go on to your home laptop/computer and find the file folder of the game
    of choice and copy it on to your USB stick.
    2) Plug in the USB to your school laptop and copy the folder onto the laptop.
    3) go into the folder, find and click on to the exe file of your game. eg Among Us.exe.
    4) enjoy your game!
